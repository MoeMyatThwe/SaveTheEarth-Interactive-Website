Personal Goals

Make our Earth a better place

Eco System

All life on Earth is supported by ecosystems. The Earth and its inhabitants will be healthier the better our ecosystems are. Ending poverty, halting climate change, and averting mass extinction are all made possible by restoring our devastated ecosystems.

Recycling

Recycling preserves natural resources and transforms garbage into valuable products. Additionally, it prolongs the life of Semakau Landfill and lessens the need for incineration. The environment can be well protected by recycling.

Save Water

Saving water reduces energy use. Your home needs energy to filter, heat, and pump water, so conserving water also lowers your carbon impact. Utilizing less water helps keep more in our ecosystems and maintains the habitats of fish, herons, water voles, and otters in wetlands.
